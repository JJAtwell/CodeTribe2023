SHEET 1: NUMBERS 1 TO 20
The participant will move their eyes across the screen as they read the numbers 1 to 20 aloud.
Each time the alarm is triggered, the number at which the alarm was triggered will be entered in the columns below.
The aim is to determine which numbers (areas on the screen) the system recognises as being drowzy.
The number with the highest frequencies also indicate weak spots in the model (screen area) in the event that the person is not really drowzy.
